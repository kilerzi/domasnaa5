    Types of Coffee Dive into popular coffee types like: Espresso: A strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground beans. Latte: Espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a thin layer of foam. Cappuccino: Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam, offering a rich and creamy flavor. Americano: Espresso diluted with hot water for a milder taste. Mocha: A chocolate-flavored variation of a latte.
